ABOUT ME

I am a senior CS major at UMass Lowell. I identify as a male (♂). I graduated from Andover High School, in the class of 2020. After immigrating to the United States from South Korea at the age of six months, I lived in Somerville, MA until I started Kindergarten. I then moved to Andover, where I reside today. For most of my life, I wasn't into coding, but I've always had an interest in technology and how things work. But during sophomore year of highschool, I took a Minecraft programming class and was instantly hooked. Some of my hobbies are long-distance running, playing video games, attending sporting events, and watching movies. When I was in high school, I was a captain of the cross country and track teams. I try to attend as many of the UML sports games as possible, and try to go to see a handful of Boston teams play each year as well. I love the Star Wars and Marvel franchises, and have watched nearly every movie and TV show in each.

My work experience consists of three years of being a summer camp counselor, two years of statistics work for a track and field website, and a summer Software Engineer internship at Raytheon.
